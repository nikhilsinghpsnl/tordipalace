tordi palace

The impressive Fort & Garh of Tordi is situated in a cosy village, tucked away in one ranges of Aravalli hills; this range covers an area of 30 kms and abounds in natural beauty. The Fort & Garh were established in the 16th century by Thakur Akhey Singhji of the Khangarot clan Rajputs. In 2010, the Palace opened its doors to guests wanting to experience the chivalrous hospitality of a Rajput chieftain and relive the glory of a bygone age. The village seems untouched by the hustle & bustle of city life. The village offers a superb opportunity to anyone who is interested in camel safaris, jeep safaris, bird watching, trekking and angling.
